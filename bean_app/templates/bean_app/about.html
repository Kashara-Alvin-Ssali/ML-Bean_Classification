About Bean Leaf Disease Classifier

Bean Leaf Disease Classifier is an AI-powered web application designed to help farmers and agricultural experts quickly and accurately identify diseases in bean leaves. Our mission is to support food security and sustainable agriculture by providing accessible, advanced technology for early disease detection.
